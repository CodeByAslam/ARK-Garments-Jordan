About Us

With 30 years of expertise, ARK Garment Manufacturing Industries Ltd. has evolved from a small factory in Sharjah, UAE into a global apparel manufacturer with operations in Jordan, Kenya, Egypt, and Oman.

Our Vision & Mission

Vision: To be the leading provider of apparel manufacturing solutions.

Mission: To produce a diverse range of garments with innovative designs, exceptional quality, and cost-effectiveness.

Our Values

✔ Commitment to on-time delivery, quality, and sustainability.

✔ High efficiency and operational excellence.

✔ Rigorous adherence to social and technical compliance.

Our Responsibility

We prioritize sustainability and responsible manufacturing with initiatives such as:

♻ Energy-efficient production to reduce environmental impact.

♻ Sustainable sourcing of materials.

♻ Waste management and recycling programs.

Our Milestones

📌 2018: Shifted operations to Jordan.

📌 2016: Opened a factory in Salalah, Oman.

📌 2011: Established a facility in Barka, Oman.

📌 2009: Launched a factory in Port Said, Egypt.

📌 2004: Expanded the Sharjah factory with 200 machines and in-house washing.

📌 2002: Entered the Kenyan market.

📌 1994: Founded as a small manufacturing unit in Sharjah, UAE.
